Examples

  • 2*(3-4)-(5+7)

Integers

  • 2 + (-3)

Fractions

  • 1/2 + 1/3

  • 1 / (a/b)

Equations

  • 2x + 5 = 10 [goal]
  • (5 - x)/3 = 2x - 7

  • 1/x + 1/y [goal]

  • x*y + x
  • x*(y + 2)
  • (x + 1)*(x - 4) [goal]

Collect Like Terms
